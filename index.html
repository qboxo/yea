Corey Qureshi is a queer writer, musician, and parent based in Philadelphia. Through poetry, fiction, and journalism, their work focuses on labor under capitalism, relationships, spirituality, identity, and art/artists in similar thematic realms. They're a Taurus that loves scifi, Nintendo, sunlight, clean spaces, and eating.
